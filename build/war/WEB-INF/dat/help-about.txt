 <tr><td colspan="2"><p>Paper Miner is a wrapper around the Australian National Library
 <a href="http://trove.nla.gov.au" target="_blank">TROVE</a> service,
 adding value by providing additional visualizations of TROVE search results,
 including geospatial data based on extensive data mining of the raw TROVE data.
 
 <p>Paper Miner is standards based and requires that your web browser support Javascript.
 It works best with Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, and Apple Safari.
 It probably won't work at all with any version of Internet Explorer.
